WHO I AM

Brazilian/Italian, 27 years old and passionate about technology.

This passion started early, as a teenager, spending hours on the computer playing games and playing with software editors of photos, videos, musics and even websites. But, as I get older, only games remained, professionally I began to aspire to the field of engineering and business administration. For this reason I decided to study industrial engineering, which I graduated in 2021.

I can't say that engineering went wrong, through it I gained a lot of logical knowledge and also a lot of theoretical and practical knowledge involving small, medium and large businesses. My first career choice was finance, as I liked numbers and understanding what they meant in practice. However, with my day to day working in finance at a multinational, I started studying programming as a hobby and fell in love with that universe. That entire universe of technology that I had always had contact as teenager with was now literally in my hands. Each day a new learning experience, a new challenge, which also brings a reward when overcome. Very different from the daily and often monotonous routine of a career in finance. That's how I had the privilege of dropping everything and going all-in on what started to make a lot of sense for what I want in my life.

Today, I'm looking for my first opportunity as a web developer and I'm constantly studying and evolving. If you want to get to know me better, chat a little or just exchange experiences, get in touch with me, it will be a pleasure.
